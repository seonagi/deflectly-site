Problem

Starting from zero, we needed a way to fill pilots for our AI support offer while working full-time and avoiding heavy sales calls.

Approach

  • Embedded our AI assistant on the site to answer questions and book pilots.
  • Sent 3-touch personalized emails to two niches (DTC skincare, med-spa/dental).
  • Self-serve 7‑day pilot page with zero‑meeting onboarding.

What changed

  • Added 10 KB articles per pilot based on top intents.
  • Escalation macros for refunds, VIPs, and sensitive topics.
  • Weekly email report with next‑best policy/KB updates.

Results

  • Median first response < 30 seconds across pilots.
  • 40–70% Tier‑1 deflection by day 7.
  • 3–6 paid conversions per 6–12 pilots started.
